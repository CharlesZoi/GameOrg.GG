3 Degrees Esports Triumphs in Cebu's First Honor of Kings Tournament

The Cebu esports scene recently witnessed a historic event as 3 Degrees Esports claimed victory in the first-ever Honor of Kings (HoK) tournament held at The Outlets at Pueblo Verde. Facing intense competition from top local teams, 3 Degrees Esports showcased exceptional skill and teamwork throughout the tournament. Their strategic gameplay and adaptability allowed them to rise above the challenges and secure the championship title. This win not only marks a significant achievement for the team but also highlights the growing esports community in Cebu, setting a new standard for future tournaments in the region.

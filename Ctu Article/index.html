ICpEP.News is dedicated to providing the latest news and updates on events and activities within the ICpEP.SE - CTU Danao Chapter. We cover key developments, student projects, and community events, keeping members connected and informed. Stay tuned as we celebrate the achievements of our organization and share insights that inspire the ICpEP community!

CPE Boys Reach Semis in National Programming Challenge

The CPE Boys from ICpEP.SE - CTU Danao have advanced to the semifinals of the National Programming Challenge hosted by CodeChum! Competing against top talents from across the country, the team demonstrated exceptional coding skills and problem-solving abilities. Their journey to the semifinals reflects their dedication, teamwork, and expertise in computer engineering. As they prepare for the next round, the entire ICpEP community is rallying behind them. Best of luck to the CPE Boys as they continue to showcase their talent on the national stage!

CPE Boys Dominate Regional C Programming and ML Tournaments

The CPE Boys from ICpEP.SE - CTU Danao have claimed victory in both the C Programming Challenge and the Mobile Legends (ML) Tournament at the regional level, earning the prestigious title of champions. Led by Mel James Calzada, who took home the C Programming Champion title, the team displayed remarkable skill in coding and gaming, outclassing their competitors in both arenas. Their hard work and teamwork have solidified their reputation as top contenders in the region. Congratulations to the CPE Boys for their outstanding achievements!

CPE Boys Shine in Mr. Pasundayag Runway Show, Bag 2nd Runner-Up

The CPE Boys proudly represented ICpEP.SE - CTU Danao in the Mr. Pasundayag runway show, where their charismatic contestant earned 2nd runner-up honors. The event was a spectacular showcase of style and confidence, with the CPE representative standing out among the crowd. In addition to their podium finish, the CPE Boys also took home the award for Best Fan Base, thanks to the overwhelming support and energy from their supporters, who outshone the other contestants. Congratulations to the CPE Boys for their impressive performance and for rallying an incredible community spirit!
